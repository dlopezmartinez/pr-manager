# Privacy Policy

**PR Manager**
**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

PR Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address (required)
- Name (optional)
- Password (stored securely using bcrypt hashing with salt)

**Payment Information**
Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor, Stripe. We do not store or have access to your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment data.

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our Service, including:
- Features used within the application
- Error reports and crash logs
- Performance metrics
- General interaction patterns

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

**Device Information**
We may collect:
- Operating system type and version
- Application version
- General device type (desktop)

### Information Stored Locally

**Git Provider Tokens**
Your GitHub or GitLab personal access tokens are:
- Stored locally on your device only
- Encrypted using OS-level encryption (Keychain on macOS, DPAPI on Windows, libsecret on Linux)
- Never transmitted to our servers
- Never shared with third parties

**Application Settings**
Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide the Service** - Create and manage your account, process subscriptions, and deliver the core functionality
2. **Improve the Service** - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
3. **Communicate with You** - Send important notices about your account, subscription, and the Service
4. **Provide Support** - Respond to your questions and requests
5. **Ensure Security** - Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- **Stripe** - Payment processing
- **Hosting providers** - Infrastructure services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide services to us.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- A subpoena or court order
- A government or regulatory request
- Protection of our legal rights
- Prevention of fraud or security threats

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:
- Your personal data will be deleted within 30 days
- Some information may be retained in anonymized form for analytics
- Payment records may be retained as required by law

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
